Our Story

Preserving Culinary Heritage Since 2010

Bodkhe Kaku Swaympak Ghar has been a cornerstone of authentic Maharashtrian cuisine in Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar for over 15 years.

Our commitment to tradition is unwavering. Every recipe we use has been perfected over generations, ensuring that each bite takes you back to the comfort of a Maharashtrian home.

Located in the heart of Nirala Bazar, we continue to serve thousands of satisfied customers who return time and again for the authentic taste that reminds them of home.
